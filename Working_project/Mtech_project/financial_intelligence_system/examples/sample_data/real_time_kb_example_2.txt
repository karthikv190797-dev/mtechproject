Title: Breaking: Apple Launches AI-Powered Financial Assistant
Timestamp: 2026-05-26T10:05:00Z
Source: Bloomberg

Apple Inc. has launched a new AI-powered financial assistant, "Apple Finance Pro," integrated into the Apple Wallet app. The assistant provides real-time spending insights, investment recommendations, and automated bill management for users in the US and Europe.

Key Features:
- Real-time transaction analysis
- Personalized investment suggestions
- Automated bill payment and reminders
- Integration with Apple Card and third-party banks

Industry Reaction:
Financial analysts predict this move will accelerate the adoption of AI in personal finance and increase competition with fintech startups. Apple shares rose 1.2% after the announcement.
