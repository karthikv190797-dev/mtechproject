TESLA, INC.
Year Ended December 31, 2023

ITEM 1. BUSINESS

Tesla, Inc. designs, develops, manufactures and sells high-performance fully
electric vehicles, energy storage systems and solar products, as well as 
related services. We leverage our software expertise and custom silicon design 
to deliver vehicles with performance, efficiency and reliability.

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS:
- Total Revenue 2023: $81.46 billion (vs $60.57 billion in 2022)
- Gross Profit: $12.59 billion (15.5% margin)
- Operating Income: $3.69 billion
- Net Income: $2.56 billion
- Operating Cash Flow: $7.86 billion

RISK FACTORS:
1. Competition in the electric vehicle market is intensifying
2. Our profitability and cash flow are heavily dependent on volume growth
3. Supply chain disruptions could impact production and delivery schedules
4. We are subject to significant government regulation and incentives
5. Prices of lithium and other battery materials have increased

RESULTS OF OPERATIONS:
The following table shows our financial results for the years 2023, 2022, and 2021:

                     2023          2022          2021
Revenue (billions):  $81.46        $60.57        $47.52
Gross Margin (%):    15.5%         27.1%         30.5%
Operating Margin (%): 4.5%         6.7%          12.5%
Net Margin (%):      3.1%          6.6%          8.2%

Key metrics by quarter (2023):
Q1: Revenue $23.32B, Operating Margin 11.4%
Q2: Revenue $24.93B, Operating Margin 9.3%
Q3: Revenue $25.18B, Operating Margin 2.7%
Q4: Revenue $25.03B, Operating Margin 1.8%

MANAGEMENT'S DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS:

The decrease in gross margin was primarily due to:
- Lower average selling prices due to increased competition
- Expansion into new markets with higher logistics costs
- Increased per-unit depreciation from new manufacturing facilities

Operating cash flow improved despite margin compression due to:
- Improved working capital management
- Increased accounts payable days
- Better inventory turns

FUTURE OUTLOOK:
We expect to continue investing heavily in:
- Expansion of manufacturing capacity
- Development of new vehicle platforms
- Energy storage and solar products
- Artificial intelligence and autonomous driving capabilities

We plan to increase production by approximately 20% in 2024 across all facilities.
