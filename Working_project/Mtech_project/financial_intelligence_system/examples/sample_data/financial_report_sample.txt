QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT
APPLE INC.
Q1 2024 (Quarter Ended December 30, 2023)
================================================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Apple delivered strong financial results in Q1 2024 with net revenue of $119.6
billion, representing 11.2% growth year-over-year. All five geographic segments
showed growth, with double-digit growth in Americas and Japan.

REVENUE BY SEGMENT (Q1 2024):

iPhone Revenue:         $69.8B (+1.1% YoY)
Mac Revenue:            $7.7B (-2.3% YoY)
iPad Revenue:           $6.5B (+4.7% YoY)
Wearables Revenue:      $13.1B (+9.7% YoY)
Services Revenue:       $22.5B (+13.9% YoY)

TOTAL NET REVENUE:      $119.6B (+11.2% YoY)

PROFITABILITY METRICS:

Gross Margin:           46.2% (vs 44.3% prior year)
Operating Margin:       30.7% (vs 28.1% prior year)
Net Income:             $29.9B
Earnings Per Share:     $1.94 (basic), $1.93 (diluted)

GEOGRAPHIC BREAKDOWN (Net Revenue):

Americas:               $59.2B (+12.3% YoY)

Rest of Asia Pacific:   $7.3B (7.2% YoY)

ANALYSIS AND INSIGHTS:

1. Services Growth Acceleration
   Services revenue growth of 13.9% demonstrates the success of our
   ecosystem expansion and customer engagement initiatives.

2. Margin Expansion
   Gross margin expansion of 190 basis points reflects improved product mix,
   manufacturing efficiency, and favorable foreign exchange rates.

3. iPhone Stability
   While iPhone grew modestly by 1.1%, the installed base remains very strong
   with high customer satisfaction and loyalty metrics.

4. Regional Performance
   Japan showed exceptional growth driven by new iPhone models and marketing
   initiatives. China growth moderated due to competitive pressures and
   macroeconomic challenges.

FORWARD GUIDANCE:

For Q2 2024, we expect:
- Sequential revenue decline typical for Q2
- Gross margin in the range of 45-46%
- Continued Services growth momentum
