1. THE "MATURITY DECELERATION" PATTERN (Apple, Google, Amazon, Netflix, Adobe)
   Characteristic: Declining growth rates in 2025 after 2024 strength
   Root Cause: Comps cycling, market saturation, macro uncertainty
   Outlook: Stabilization at single-digit growth rates
   Investment: Lower risk, lower return profile

2. THE "RECOVERY TRAJECTORY" PATTERN (Intel, Tesla - but Tesla struggling)
   Characteristic: Strong recovery from 2024 lows continuing into 2025
   Intel Success: +11.0% sustained in Jun 2025 vs -7.2% Apr 2024
   Tesla Challenge: Moderated to +3.4%, no longer accelerating
   Outlook: Intel: Continued acceleration potential; Tesla: Plateauing
   Investment: Intel higher risk/reward, Tesla more defensive

3. THE "NORMALIZATION FROM EXTREMES" PATTERN (NVIDIA)
   Characteristic: Unsustainable growth rates returning to market average
  Peak: +217.9% (June 2024) - clearly unsustainable
   Current: +5.0% (June 2025) - market growth equivalent
   Nature: Expected mean reversion, not failure
   Investment: Risk of further compression to negative if cycle completes

4. THE "GROWTH CLIFF" PATTERN (Meta)
   Characteristic: Rapid reversal from +27.3% to +1.5% in 6 months
   Severity: -25.8 percentage point decline - steepest among cohort
   Cause: Both comps cycling AND underlying demand deceleration visible
   Risk: Might continue declining if trend accelerates
   Investment: Caution warranted unless deceleration stabilizes

5. THE "STEADY EDDIE" PATTERN (Microsoft)
   Characteristic: Consistent moderate growth throughout period
   Range: +8.3% (low) to +21.5% (peak) - relatively narrow
   Quarterly Pattern: Stable regardless of seasonality
   Reliability: Most predictable company in cohort
   Investment: Low risk, reasonable return profile
