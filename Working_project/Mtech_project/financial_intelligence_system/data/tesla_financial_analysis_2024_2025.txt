CONCLUSION
==========
Tesla's 2024-2025 performance shows a company managing through a transition period. The dramatic decline of Q1 2024 created investor anxiety, but the recovery through 2024 demonstrated resilience and operational excellence. However, 2025 trends suggest the explosive growth phase of 2020-2023 has moderated as the company matures.

The critical inflection point for Tesla is successfully scaling the Energy business to 15%+ of revenue while maintaining competitive pricing in the Auto segment. The upcoming next-gen platform launch and potential robotaxi deployment offer significant long-term upside, but near-term execution on these initiatives will determine investor sentiment.

Current valuation appears to price in modest growth assumptions. Upside depends on successful energy scaling and robotaxi commercialization. Downside risks include persistent EV price competition and macro weakness affecting luxury vehicle demand.

Recommendation: Monitor quarterly gross margins closely. If margins compress below 20%, growth momentum may be unsustainable. Watch energy segment growth trajectory - if it achieves 30%+ YoY growth, it becomes company's growth engine by 2026.
